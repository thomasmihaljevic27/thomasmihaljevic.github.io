About Me

I am a Master of Arts in Economics candidate at Lakehead University, focused on applying econometric models to real-world challenges in Northern Ontario. My academic work bridges the gap between raw data and actionable policy, with a specific interest in population retention and regional development.

Outside of policy, I apply these same statistical rigors to sports analytics. I am currently developing models to assess trade efficiency in the NHL, exploring the "Process vs. Results" dilemma in rookie player evaluation.

When I am not in Stata or Notion, you can find me working on interior design projects or optimizing my home office setup.
